# 🛒 Grocery@Official E-Commerce

Welcome to **Grocery@Official**, your one-stop online grocery store! 🍎🥦🛍️ Discover fresh produce, pantry essentials, meats, and more with our easy-to-use platform. Enjoy free delivery, quick search, and seamless shopping experience right from your browser.

## 🌟 Key Features
- **🏠 Home Page**: Order your daily groceries with our intuitive search banner.
- **📂 Categories**: Browse through various categories like fruits, vegetables, meats, and utensils.
- **🌟 Popular Products**: Explore top-selling items and bundle packs.
- **💬 Testimonials**: Read what our satisfied customers say.
- **🤝 Partners**: Trusted by leading brands.
- **📱 Mobile App**: Download our app for Android and iOS.
- **🛒 Shopping Cart & Wishlist**: Add items to cart or save for later with heart icons.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
- **HTML5** for structure
- **CSS3** for styling (with Font Awesome icons)
- **JavaScript** for interactivity
- **Google Fonts** (Poppins) for typography

## 🚀 How to Run
1. Clone or download the project files.
2. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
3. Start shopping! 🛒

Feel free to explore and enjoy fresh groceries delivered to your doorstep! 🌽🍓
